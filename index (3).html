About Us

Our Story

Founded in January 2025 after a divine encounter by Minister Anthony Mwendwa, Ignited Generation began as a small prayer cell of young believers hungry for more of God.

Through powerful encounters with the Holy Spirit, we've grown into a nationwide mission force, reaching thousands of young people across Kenya with the fire of the Gospel.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Igniting hearts for Jesus, transforming lives through radical evangelism, discipleship, and Spirit-led encounters. We exist to see young people set ablaze with passion for Christ and equipped to spread His fire wherever they go.

Our Vision

A Spirit-filled generation transforming Kenya and the world—one city, one soul at a time. We envision campuses, streets, and neighborhoods across Africa burning with revival fires as young people walk in power and purpose.
